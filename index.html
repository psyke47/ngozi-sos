My Personal Story

Inspiration

  • On the 29th of January 2025, I was mugged twice at gunpoint in the span of 15 minutes by the same group of criminals.
  • In the first attack, I saw them coming but had no way to call for immediate help.
  • After failing to get assistance from passing motorists, they returned, dragged me into the bushes, and held me hostage at gunpoint while forcing me to give up my bank card PIN, with a gun put to my head while one of the guys went to the nearest ATM to withdraw from my accounts.
  • If I had a tool like NgoziSOS, I could have alerted armed response before the attack, drawing security attention to my location.

Realisation

  • Current security systems protect cars and buildings, but not individuals.
  • Emergency response times are too slow, leaving victims vulnerable.
  • NgoziSOS was born to fill this urgent gap in personal safety.

Problem Statement – The Need for Instant Security Response

  • High crime rates make pedestrians and commuters vulnerable.
  • Police response times are slow due to lack of resources and personnel.
  • Women and children are the most vulnerable in society, often unable to seek help discreetly without increasing their risk.
  • No existing solution focuses on individual safety in real-time.
  • Medical emergencies require faster intervention, but existing services are fragmented.
  • Lack of coordination between private security firms and emergency responders.

The Solution – What is NgoziSOS?

NgoziSOS is a mobile panic button & emergency alert platform that:

  • Sends an instant distress alert to nearby armed response teams.
  • GPS-tracks users in real-time, ensuring rapid location detection.
  • Allows users to request emergency medical services with auto-synced medical aid details.
  • Creates a secure environment where security companies, medical responders, and users work together.
  • Uses a subscription-based model to ensure long-term affordability.
