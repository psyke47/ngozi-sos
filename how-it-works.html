How NgoziSOS Works

Your safety is just a tap away. Learn how our platform connects you to emergency response teams in real-time.

How It Works – Simple & Effective

  1. User triggers the SOS button upon sensing a threat.
  2. Real-time location alert is sent to partnered security firms in the area.
  3. Armed response dispatches units to assist the user.
  4. For medical emergencies, EMS providers receive instant patient details.
  5. Users can link medical aid or opt for premium emergency coverage.

Key Features

Emergency SOS Alert System

One-tap distress signal with real-time tracking.

Automated Armed Response Dispatch

Nearest security firms receive alerts.

Medical Emergency Assistance

Connects users with ambulances & medical providers.

Geo-fencing & Crime Alerts

Users receive warnings when entering high-risk areas.

Silent Mode Activation

Users can discreetly trigger alerts without drawing attention.

Real-Life Scenarios Where NgoziSOS Can Save Lives

A Woman on an Unsafe Date

Sarah meets someone for a first date but begins feeling uncomfortable. She discreetly activates the Silent Mode SOS, which sends her GPS location to a trusted contact and a nearby security response team. Armed response arrives within minutes, ensuring her safety without escalating the situation.

A Child Walking Home from School

10-year-old Lunga notices an unknown vehicle following him on his way home. He presses the panic button, and his live location is shared with security services and his registered guardian. Within minutes, a security patrol intercepts and ensures his safe return home.

A Commuter Facing an Attempted Mugging

Thabo is walking to the taxi rank when he sees a group of suspicious individuals approaching. He activates the SOS button, which alerts the closest armed response team. The patrol arrives before the attackers can act, preventing a robbery.

A Medical Emergency in a Public Place

Mary suddenly collapses from an asthma attack at a shopping mall. Bystanders activate NgoziSOS’s medical emergency feature, immediately notifying paramedics with her medical details. She receives treatment in time, preventing a critical situation.
